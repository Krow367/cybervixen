Recipes

These are some of my favorite recipes that I've collected over the years. Some of them can be done with just a basic oven range while others are meant for cooking on a grill or griddle. Unless otherwise noted, assume you can make each recipe in a standard home kitchen.

Oh, and don't worry. None of these will have the pointlessly long novels before the recipe to create more space for advertisements found on most sites. Just a quick few notes and then the good stuff.
