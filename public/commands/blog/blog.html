May 25 2026

What a hassle

Decided to finally switch my laptop from windows to linux. Of course, things the way they are, it couldn't be as simple as just installing linux and going about my day. Turns out that there's no native support for the Mediatek MT2907 network card! Windows only. And guess which network card this little fox's laptop has? Yep. Doesn't help that they don't have ethernet ports on laptops anymore. Gotta keep that slim fucking form factor, y'know? Thankfully I was able to use my phone as the USB tethered hotspot to get internet to the laptop. Took a few different attempts but I was also able to track down drivers that some blessed individual made. I love nerds of all flavors.

Beyond all that, rented a carpet cleaner today. Going to turn the laundry room into a craft room of sorts. Get my sewing machine and serger set up. Hoping that I'll be able to start making my own clothes. If you ignore the costs of labor, its technically cheaper than buying them! Not really. But homemade clothes are more durable than the fast fashion trash that's peddled today.

Also... It's Memorial Day here in the states today. I hate this day. It's nothing more than an excuse for people to have a cookout or go camping for a weekend. On the rare occassion that someone actually remembers what Memorial Day is supposed to be about, they come up to me with their "Thank you for your service" bullshit. Bitch, I'm still alive. This ain't my day. Memorial Day is for the ones that we've lost. You wanna thank a Vet? November 11. We got out own day.

If you have family or friends that you've lost in the line of duty? Please, take a few moments today to talk about them to those around you. Tell those funny stories. Maybe talk about how you met them. Let their memory come alive for a little bit today. That's how you honor the fallen. Not with barbeques and fucking sales. Pour one out. Talk. Laugh. But most importantly... remember. And stop thanking those of us still here. We ain't dead yet.

Not much else to say, I guess. Still have more recipes to get up on the site here. Also need to figure out how I want to design this place. What I have now technically works, but it's boring. Still, early days and all that. I do have a couple ideas, I just need to figure out how its actually done.
