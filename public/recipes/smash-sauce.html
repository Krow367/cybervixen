Smash Burger Sauce


Click here for printer friendly version!

While I originally developed this sauce to be used on smash burgers with my griddle, there's no reason to limit it to just burgers! It also makes a fantastic dipping sauce for fries, or even fried chicken!

This recipe makes enough sauce for about a dozen or so burgers. It'll keep in the fridge for a bit in a covered container. I can't speak to how long it'll last as it's usually gone in about a week whenever I make it.

If you like Horseradish, include the white vinegar. If you're a normal person, definitely leave it out as even a little bit will ruin the entire sauce.



Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Mayo
  • 1 tbsp Ketchup
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard (I recommend Grey Poupon)
  • 2 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp sweet relish
  • 1/4 tsp white Vinegar (optional)
