#Shrimp and Okra Gumbo

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces kielbasa sausages, halved lengthwise and cut into 1/4-inch halfmoons

  • 3 celery stalks, 1/4-inch slices

  • 6 green onions

  • 8 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 cup chicken broth

  • 14 oz diced tomatoes

  • 1 pound large raw deveined tailless shrimp

  • 2 cups cut okra (if using frozen, let it fully thaw first, otherwise you will drag the temperature down and it won’t cook right! Same goes for the shrimp above)

  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped

  • 1 medium white or sweet onion, chopped

  • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • 2 tsp Cajun seasoning

  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme

Directions

  1. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat. Add Kielbasa and brown (~four minutes). Add to crock pop.

  2. Add bell pepper, chopped onion, celery, green onions and garlic, cook for ~6 minutes until veggies are crisp-tender. Add to crock pot.

  3. In crock pot, stir in broth, tomatoes, seasoning, and thyme.

  4. Cover; cook on LOW 4 hours. Stir in shrimp and okra. Cover; cook on LOW 1 hour.
