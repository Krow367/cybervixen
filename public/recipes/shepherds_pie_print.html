Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 cans condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can drained cut green beans
  • 24oz mashed potatoes
  • 2 cups shredded cheese


    • Directions

    1. If using pre-made, heat them up according to directions. Preheat oven to 350°F
    2. Brown ground beef in skillet
    3. Mix the beef, cream of mushrooms and green beans in round casserole dish
    4. Top with mashed potatoes, leaving a hole in the center
    6. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
    7. Remove from oven and top with shredded cheese, then bake for another 5-10 minutes until cheese is melted
    8. Serve and enjoy!
