Ham and Cheese Delight

Click here for Printer Friendly Version!

It's ham and cheese and egg and it's delightful. It's quick, it's easy. Make it. Enjoy it. Make it again. Eat it for breakfast or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked diced ham
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2/3 cup finely crushed saltine crackers
  • 3 lightly beaten eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp pepper (or to taste)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Sautee onions with butter until tender
  3. Add all other ingredients, mix well
  4. Pour mixture into baking dish
  5. Bake 45-50 minutes or until knife in center comes out clean
  6. Enjoy!
