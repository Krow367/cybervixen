Fall Harvest Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds cubed beef stew meat

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • 1 tbsp butter

  • 1 medium white or red onion, chopped

  • 1 full bulb garlic, chopped

  • 2 whole bay leaves

  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped

  • 1 1/2 tsp fresh thyme, chopped

  • 1/2 cup beef broth, divided

  • 1 pound carrots, cut into 2-inch pieces

  • 4 turnips, cut into 1-inch pieces

  • 1 butternut squash, cut into 2-inch pieces

  • 12 ounce (1 can) sout, divided

  • 1/4 tsp white pepper

  • dash apple pie spice

  • salt and black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Add flour and beef to large bowl; toss to coat. Heat oil and butter in skillet over medium-high heat. Brown beef on all sides. Remove beef to slow cooker (leave any juices in skillet)

  2. Return skillet to heat. Add onion, garlic, bay leaves, rosemary and thyme; cook until onion begins to soften. Add to slow cooker.

  3. Use half 1/4 cup broth to deglaze skillet. Add deglazed mixture and all other ingredients to slow cooker. Cover; cook on LOW 8 hours. Remove bay leaves before serving.
