Pizza Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 lb Ground Beef
  • 28 oz pizza sauce of choice (I recommend Rao's homemade pizza sauce)
  • 8 oz dry wt jar of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp Garlic Salt (don't overdo this! In fact, if you're sensitive to salt you might want to leave the garlic salt out all together)
  • 1 tbsp (at least) Garlic Powder
  • 2 cups dry rotini pasta (doesn't need to be rotini, but that's what Grandma used)
  • 2/3 cup milk (whole, 2%, doesn't matter)
  • 16 oz shredded Mozzarella
  • 6 oz (at least) Pepperoni
  • Anything else you like! Bell peppers, onion, etc. Go crazy!



  1. Start heating pot of water for pasta. Do not add dry pasta until step 4. Pre-heat oven to 350°F.
  2. In skillet, brown ground beef; drain.
  3. Stir in pizza sauce, UNdrained mushrooms, oerago, garlic, and other seasonings.
    • If you're adding other things such as the green pepper or onion, add them now.
  4. Bring sauce mixture to simmer. Add rotiini to water. Cook both for 7 minutes.
  5. Drain pasta, then combine with sauce mixture and milk.
  6. Add half the sauce mix to casserole dish, then layer with cheese and pepperoni. Repeat with second half.
  7. Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes.
  8. Eat!
