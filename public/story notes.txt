World setting: Alternate reality game. IBM never released the PC. Instead, Serenity Industries dominates the market and makes almost every computer and OS. The player will have to fix the corruption in foxOS to discover its secrets, as well as the mysteries surrounding Serenity Industries and what that may mean for the world....

Year: 1981, divergence point from real history

foxOS — suppressed OS that perceives simulation

CyberVixen — whistleblower, identity unknown

Project: Foxhound — first clue

DEMIURGE — SI's firewall, final obstacle

The reveal — SI administers a simulated world, foxOS can see it and break through to other world nodes. This starts the ultimate endgame.

Game steps and mechanics:

[x]Repair game reveals Foxhound floppy disk and fixed help file which contains foxclaw command.

[ ]foxclaw first run gives a permission denied error. Hints Foxhound credentials required.

[ ]load Foxhound command launches next minigame, a breakout clone.

[ ]Winning foxhound game unlocks CyberVixen message containing warning of danger and instructions of unlocked foxclaw commands. foxclaw.scan(local) reveals one connected node as DEMIURGE that cannot be attacked at all. It's a massive firewall surrounding Serenity Industries network.
